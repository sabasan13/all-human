About

On the debut album, ‘May There Be Peace’, Vonpsyche [vɔnˈsaɪki] masterfully synthesizes the matter from the intangible realms that are rooted deep in the transpersonal dimensions. The opening track ‘Dice’ straight away introduces the songwriting jugglery that embeds the personal in the timeless context. This unfolds even more in the next track Jupiter, adding the heaviness of musical production to the lyrical artistry. ‘Hypnosis’ bridges the listener into the deep meditative state, preparing him to transit to the ecstatic feeling of the massive closing track Source.

Bringing all together the context of timelessness, synchrony, immortality, future, life, death, and God, the album title is inspired by the John Coltrane chant, and that hints at what this album for its author really is, - a prayer. Stream "May There Be Peace" on every major streaming platform around the world on 14 October 2022.
